# Revenue Model — Sharper Man

## Business Model

Sharper Man is a **freemium subscription app**. The free tier is designed as an acquisition channel — it gives users enough to get invested but withholds the features that create genuine transformation. Premium unlocks the full discipleship OS.

**The core principle:** Sharper Man must pay for itself. It cannot cost more than it makes. Every infrastructure decision, every feature choice, and every launch strategy reflects this constraint.

---

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | Hook tier. Gets men in the door. |
| Premium Monthly | $7.99/mo | Full access, billed monthly. |
| Premium Annual | $59.99/yr | Full access, billed annually. Saves 37%. |
| Premium+ Monthly *(Phase 3)* | $14.99/mo | Phase 3 features + all premium. |
| Premium+ Annual *(Phase 3)* | $119.99/yr | Phase 3 full access, billed annually. |

### Pricing Rationale

**$7.99/month** is positioned below most fitness apps ($12–20) and above most Bible apps ($5). It's the "serious man" price — not cheap enough to feel disposable, not expensive enough to create friction. At annual pricing, the effective rate is $5/month, which feels like a genuine deal.

At $7.99/month, the break-even point (infrastructure only) is **315 paid subscribers**. That's achievable from the initial email list and church community without any paid acquisition.

## Free Tier Design

The free tier is deliberately limited but genuinely useful:

| Free Feature | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| Bible reader (KJV/ASV/WEB) | Gets the app on their phone as a daily Bible |
| One rotating track/month | Hooks them with one track, frustrates them with the paywall on the next |
| Daily reading plan + AI commentary | Shows what premium commentary could feel like |
| Basic streak tracking | Creates habit attachment |

**What free does NOT include:**
- Elijah (the AI coach) — this is the single most compelling premium feature
- Voice narration — men immediately want this after seeing it mentioned
- Health tracking — creates curiosity about the whole-man dashboard
- Accountability partner — deeply appealing once they understand it
- Prayer wall (Phase 2) — community features drive conversion

## Paywall Trigger Moments

Paywalls are shown at intentional moments, not randomly:

| Moment | Paywall Trigger |
|--------|----------------|
| User finishes the free track | "You've completed [Track]. 12 more await in Premium." |
| User taps "Start Check-In" | "Elijah requires Premium. Here's what he'd say to you today..." |
| User taps "Listen" on any track | "Voice narration is a Premium feature." |
| User views progress dashboard | "Unlock your three-pillar tracking dashboard." |
| User opens Prayer Wall (Phase 2) | "Submit and browse prayer requests with Premium." |
| Day 7 of app usage (free) | Contextual email: "You've been with us a week. Here's what Premium unlocks." |

## Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (4–6 weeks before app store submission)
1. Email existing list (~3,000): announce Sharper Man, collect early interest
2. Post to social media (church community + personal following): tease the concept
3. Create a simple landing page (sharperman.com): email capture + "Notify me at launch"
4. Offer a **Founding Member** pricing: $4.99/month locked for life for the first 200 subscribers

### Launch Week
1. Submit to App Store + Play Store
2. Email list: "Sharper Man is live" with direct download link
3. Announce in church men's ministry
4. Personal outreach to 20–30 men who would be ideal early adopters
5. Offer 30-day free trial of Premium to first 500 downloads

### Post-Launch (ongoing)
1. Monthly new Bible study track — gives email list a reason to open every communication
2. Monthly progress report emails for Premium users — high-value touchpoint
3. Accountability partner emails — organic viral loop (see below)

## The Viral Loop

The accountability partner system is the primary organic growth mechanic:

```
Premium User sets up accountability partner
           ↓
Partner receives email: "Your friend set you up on Sharper Man"
           ↓
Partner gets periodic pattern alerts (when user deteriorates)
           ↓
Partner reads about the app, sees the value
           ↓
Partner downloads the app
           ↓
Partner converts to Premium
           ↓
Partner sets up their own accountability partner
           ↓
Repeat
```

Every Premium subscriber generates ~1 qualified warm lead (their accountability partner). No paid acquisition required at launch. With 300 paying subscribers at launch, that's ~300 warm leads already in the ecosystem.

## Retention Strategy

A subscriber who is actively using Elijah, tracking all three pillars, and has an accountability partner set up is **extremely unlikely to cancel**. The switching cost is high — their history, their patterns, their partner relationship all live in Sharper Man.

Key retention drivers:
- **Streak psychology** — no man wants to break a 90-day streak
- **Living profile continuity** — Elijah knows your story; starting over elsewhere is a real cost
- **Accountability partner setup** — canceling means explaining yourself to your partner
- **Monthly progress report** — a monthly reminder of tangible growth
- **New track cadence** — fresh content every month prevents content exhaustion

Target 12-month retention rate: **65–75%** (vs. industry average of ~40–50% for faith apps)

---

## Founding Member Offer

For the first 200 subscribers, offer a Founding Member rate: **$4.99/month, locked forever.**

This creates urgency, rewards early adopters, and builds a core of deeply invested users who will evangelize the app. The revenue sacrifice (~$900/mo vs full price from these 200 users) is worth the word-of-mouth flywheel.

Lock this offer to exactly 200 users. After that, standard pricing only.
